我家门前有一棵树。

春天，她沉睡着不肯醒来，其他的树已经郁郁葱葱，她小芽才露。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1209" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree1/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1209" title="tree1" src="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree1.jpg?w=500" alt="tree1" width="500" height="375" /></a>

夏天，她终于也长满了叶，但是毫不起眼，和其他的树一样，默默地当背景。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1210" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree2/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1210" title="tree2" src="http://azaleasays.files.wordpress.com/2009/10/tree2.jpg?w=500" alt="tree2" width="500" height="375" /></a>

秋天，她似乎想表达些什么。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1211" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree3/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1211" title="tree3" src="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree3.jpg?w=375" alt="tree3" width="375" height="500" /></a>

然而，提早来袭的大雪使她抬不起头。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1212" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree4/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1212" title="tree4" src="http://azaleasays.files.wordpress.com/2009/10/tree4.jpg?w=375" alt="tree4" width="375" height="500" /></a>

她不能逃避，甚至不能抖落些许身上的重负。然而她没有放弃希望。

风雪过后，她终于在阳光下绽放。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1213" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree5/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1213" title="tree5" src="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree5.jpg?w=375" alt="tree5" width="375" height="500" /></a>

众人的目光，也聚焦在她身上。（那天我同学来我家，说你这里好漂亮，跟风景名胜区似的。我说，你只是说那个树漂亮吧。她说，是啊。）

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1214" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree6/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1214" title="tree6" src="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree6.jpg?w=500" alt="tree6" width="500" height="375" /></a>

虽然，她知道冬天的脚步近了，将带走她夺目的光彩。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1215" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree7/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1215" title="tree7" src="http://azaleasays.files.wordpress.com/2009/10/tree7.jpg?w=500" alt="tree7" width="500" height="375" /></a>

但是，她无畏寒冷，在沉默中积蓄着力量。因为她知道，来年自己将更加绚烂。

<a rel="attachment wp-att-1216" href="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg2009/10/20/a-tree/tree8/"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1216" title="tree8" src="http://azaleasays.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/tree8.jpg?w=500" alt="tree8" width="500" height="332" /></a>

（感谢pala和alex同学友情提供照片。这些照片很多都是不经意间拍的，没想到连起来竟然概括了一棵树的四季。虽然只是一棵树，但是她已经是我们生活的一部分。）
